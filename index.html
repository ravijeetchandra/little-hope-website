Our Programs

Learning Through Wonder, Not Workbooks.

At Little Hope, the most profound learning happens during play. Our child-led model uses play-based activities incorporating nature, art, and exploration to make every concept come alive.

Ages 2 – 3 Years

Sensory Exploration & Motor Skills

Building confidence, mastering basic self-help skills, and developing sensory processing through joyful guided play.

  • ✓Guided sensory bins and finger painting
  • ✓Climbing structures and balance activities
  • ✓Fine motor skills — stacking, grasping, threading
  • ✓Large motor skills — running, balancing, jumping
  • ✓Rhythm-based play and music exploration
  • ✓Goal: Curiosity, independence, sensory integration
Ages 4 – 5 Years

Cognitive & Language Mastery

Preparing for formal schooling while maintaining a playful, exploratory spirit and love of learning.

  • ✓Dramatic play (mini-store, role-playing) for numeracy
  • ✓Interactive storytelling and phonics
  • ✓Math concepts via manipulatives (not worksheets)
  • ✓Structured art projects for creative expression
  • ✓Social-emotional leadership development
  • ✓Goal: Critical thinking, literacy, and leadership
