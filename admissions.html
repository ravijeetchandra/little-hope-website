Key Admission Information

Who We Are For

  • Children aged 2.5 to 5.5 years
  • Toddler Program: Ages 2-3
  • Pre-K Program: Ages 4-5

Operational Hours

  • Drop-off: 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Pick-up: 3:30 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Admin Hours: Mon-Fri, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

The Little Hope Promise

  • Meticulously balanced environment
  • Safety with inspiration
  • Transparent fee structure
  • Flexible payment options

Documents Required

  • Child's birth certificate
  • Proof of residence

  • Medical records/immunization
